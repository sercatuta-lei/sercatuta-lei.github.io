The Software Engineering Research Group supervised by Dr. Jeff Lei is part of Software Engineering Research Center (SERC) in the Department of [Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)](https://www.cse.uta.edu) at the [University of Texas at Arlington (UTA)](https://www.uta.edu). Our group conducts cutting-edge research in various areas of software engineering, including software design, specification, analysis, verification, and testing.
