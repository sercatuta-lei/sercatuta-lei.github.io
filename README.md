# SERC Lab Website

This is the website of our academic research group at University of Texas at Arlington.

[Group meeting records](https://github.com/sercatuta-lei/group-archive.git)

This website is powered by Jekyll and some Bootstrap, Bootwatch. It was adapted using the wonderful template provided by the Allan Lab at *https://www.allanlab.org/aboutwebsite.html*.

It's also welcome to re-use our template for your purpose. Please refer [About page](https://sercatuta-lei.github.io/aboutwebsite.html)
